Why?

Cruxly can analyze Twitter, Facebook, other social media posts, as well as email messages to detect actionable intent in near real-time. Using proprietary data analytics, Cruxly identifies a Want (Likes and Buying Intent), Question or Request, or a Promise or Commitment. Cruxly helps businesses in their marketing and sales initiatives including lead generation, product management and research, and customer service.

Patented technology

Cruxly can analyze the content of any online post or message using patented analytics to detect wants, likes, questions, and promises. The Cruxly team collectively has more than 50 years of NLP experience, and is led by Aloke Guha who holds 6 patents (of 25 issued) related to machine learning and data mining.

Designed for social media

Available in various flavors

HTTP API

The easiest and most common usage is to call our HTTP API from your application that receives or send messages. The API will process the message body you provide and return JSON with actionable text, questions or commitments. .

Android library

Worried about security? Integrate Cruxly's core library from your Android app and dont worry about sending personal data to any servers.

.Net library

Leverage Cruxly's detection logic by embedding it within your desktop application. To prove it, we are shipping a standalone Microsoft Outlook add-in that requires no communication with Cruxly servers.
